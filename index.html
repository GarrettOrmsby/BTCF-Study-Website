Study Information

About the Study

The purpose of this study is to prevent a serious dental disease called early childhood caries that can occur in very young children. The study will take place over four years. It will begin during your pregnancy and follow your baby through age three.

The primary activities that will be done for the study include:

  • Completing questionnaires
  • Watching videos
  • Short dental screenings

The four in-person visits will include questionnaires and a video to watch covering the oral health of you and your baby. The first visit will occur during pregnancy and will only involve the questionnaires and video. Once your child reaches one year of age, the next three in-person study visits will also involve a simple and FREE dental screening for your child conducted by a University of Iowa dental provider to check for cavities and collect a small sample of plaque with a cotton swab.

Between each in-person study visit we will send you questionnaires to complete online. These questionnaires will be sent out twice, once around one month after your in-person visit and again at nine months. You will be compensated for each online questionnaire you complete. Three months after your in-person visit, we will send you a short video to watch.

Study Timeline

The "Birth to Three-Cavity Free" study takes place over four years. The first in-person study visit will be scheduled when you are between 16 to 36 weeks pregnant. We will schedule the next three study visits when your child is approximately 1, 2, and 3 years of age. These yearly visits will take place at your local WIC clinic. Between each yearly study visit, there will be three online research activities. These activities will be sent out through text and email.

Your participation in this study is much appreciated as we hope to keep your child and so many other children cavity free!

FAQ

Q. When do I receive compensation?

A. You will recieve your gift card right after the in-person study visit. After each completed online activitiy, we will mail you check to your provided address.

Q. What should I do if I cannot make it for my scheduled study visit appointment?

A. We understand that life is busy! Please call us at 319-467-4121 or email us at dentistry-cavityfree-study@uiowa.edu as soon as possible, so we can reschedule your appointment.

Q. What should I do if my contact information, such as phone number, email, or address, change?

A. Please call us at 319-467-4121 or email us at dentistry-cavityfree-study@uiowa.edu to update your contact information. This is very important, so we can get a hold of you to schedule future visits and/or answer any questions you may have about the study.

Q. What will happen if I am not eligible for WIC anymore?

A. Once you are part of our study, we will continue to see you and your child even if you are not a WIC participant anymore.
