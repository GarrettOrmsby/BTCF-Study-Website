Study Information

About the Study

The purpose of the study is to prevent a serious dental disease called early childhood caries that can occur in very young children. The study will begin during your pregnancy and will follow your baby through age three.

The primary activities that will be done for the study include filling out simple questionnaires and watching videos. Some of these activities will be done in person at the local WIC clinic and others can be completed online. Once the child reaches one year of age the inperson visits will involve a simple and free dental screening to check for plaque and cavities. (Mention dental screenings for siblings?)

Compensation

Study Timeline

The "Birth to Three Cavity Free" study takes place over four years. The first year begins at pregnancy, and the following visits will take place a year apart when the child is one, two, and three years old. The yearly visits will take place at the WIC clinic. Between each yearly visit there will be three online research activities. These activities will be sent out through text and email. A set of questionnaires will be sent out one month after your inperson visit. A short video will be sent out three months following your visit, and one more set of questionnaires at nine months.
