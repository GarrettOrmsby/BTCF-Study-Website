About the Study

The "Birth to Three Cavity Free" research study aims to understand dental health in children from birth to age three. Our goal is to prevent cavities and promote better oral health practices in young children.

Study Timeline

  • Phase 1: Enrollment & Initial Health Assessment (Jan - Mar)
  • Phase 2: Regular Health Check-ups (Apr - Aug)
  • Phase 3: Final Review & Results Sharing (Sep - Dec)

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate?

Families with children aged 0-3 years are eligible to participate in the study.

Is there a cost to participate?

No, participation is free for all eligible families.

How do I enroll?

You can enroll by contacting us through the form below or by calling our office.
